For Submissions

Submitted documents should be in the form of a Word or Google document (no PDFs). In order to more easily faciliate a blind selection process, please remove any identifying personal information from your document. MUSE prefers essay submissions to be double-spaced, 12-point font, and in Chicago style (note-bibliography citation format), though other academic styles (MLA, APA) are accepted.

In order to more easily faciliate a blind selection process, please remove any identifying personal information from your document.

MUSE accepts submissions on a rolling basis; however, essays must be recieved by the end of Week 1 in order to be considered for that quarter's issue. Any essays received after Week 1 of each quarter will automatically be considered for the next quarter's issue. If selected, you will be contacted via the email provided. You may submit no more than two essays for consideration per quarter.

All submissions should be submitted using the form to the right. If you have any questions concerning the submission process, please feel free to contact us at uclamusejournal@gmail.com.
