For Submissions

We accept submissions for our Fall variety issue.

Submissions are due by Friday of week 1 of Fall quarter.

Each article will be reviewed by our Editorial Board before a final review from a graduate student in musicology.

For citations and formatting, please use Chicago style, notes and bibliography system.

We reserve the right to alter or change formatting to fit the standards of the journal.

If you are accepted to the journal, a two-sentence bio must be submitted before the beginning of week 3.

If a student was previously featured in the previous issue, they may still submit to the variety issue.

If a student submits to the previous issue and is denied, they may apply again to the variety issue.

Please read previous issues of the journal to develop a sense of the style and quality we are looking for in submissions.

All submissions should be sent to uclamusejournal@gmail.com.

For questions, please use the form to the right.
