Submission Instructions

MUSE accepts submissions on a rolling basis; however, essays must be received by the end of Week 1 in order to be considered for that quarter's issue. Any essays received after Week 1 of each quarter will automatically be considered for the next quarter's issue. If selected, you will be contacted via the email provided. You may submit no more than two essays for consideration per quarter.

Submitted documents should be in the form of a Word or Google document (no PDFs). In order to help facilitate our blind selection process, please remove any identifying personal information from your document. MUSE prefers essay submissions to be double-spaced, 12-point font, and in Chicago style (note-bibliography citation format), though other academic styles (MLA, APA) are accepted.

All submissions should be submitted here. If you have any questions concerning the submission process, please feel free to contact us at uclamusejournal@gmail.com.

Working with MUSE

If you are interested in getting involved with MUSE, we invite you to consider applying for one of the journal’s staff positions. For the 2020-2021 academic year, we are accepting applications for editors, graphic designers, web designers, and marketing team members. Applications are open to any undergraduate student at UCLA, regardless of major. Previous experience in editing/publishing is not required. The application form can be found here. If selected for a staff position, you will be notified via email during Fall Quarter.

*The deadline for staff applications for the 2020-2021 academic year is October 16, 2020. For more information about applying to MUSE, please contact uclamusejournal@gmail.com.
